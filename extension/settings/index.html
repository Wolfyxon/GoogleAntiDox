Mode

Your location will be hidden. Randomization will be available in future updates.

Note

This extension does not protect your location from Google. It only hides the elements that show your location so you don't accidentally show it in a screenshot, recording or a screenshare.

Please also note that it doesn't protect you from location-based results (for example when you google "shops" and the results show shops near you).
